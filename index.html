I am an AI Researcher at Labelbox, Applied Machine Learning Research, working on model evaluation and AI safety.

I earned my PhD in Computer Science from the University of Arizona, advised by Prof. Mihai Surdeanu. My research interests include Machine Learning, Large Language Models (LLMs), and Natural Language Processing. I am also interested in stress-testing LLMs to challenge AI safety and evaluation.

My PhD dissertation was the first to identify the issue of data contamination (data leakage) in LLMs, where training data overlaps with evaluation data. I developed several methods to detect and estimate contamination in fully black-box LLMs. My PhD research received media coverage and earned several awards, including the Outstanding Graduate Scholarship and the Galileo Circle Scholarship.

Previously, I interned at Google Cloud AI Research, Walmart Global Tech, and Harvard Medical School.
